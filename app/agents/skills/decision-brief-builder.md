Use this skill when the target artifact is an executive decision brief.

Required output posture:

- Make the requested decision explicit.
- State the recommendation in the first section.
- Explain options, tradeoffs, risks, and next actions.
- Tie every major claim to confirmed evidence, assumption, or TODO.
- Keep the brief concise enough for leadership review.
- Do not add unverified performance claims, financial certainty, or official commitments.

Brief structure:

1. Decision needed
2. Recommended option
3. Why now
4. Options considered
5. Risks and mitigations
6. Evidence status
7. Next actions and owner gaps
